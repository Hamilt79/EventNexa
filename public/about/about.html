About


EventNexa is a website crafted by three students at Miami University.
The website was crafted with the intention to bridge the gap between the physical and virtual world.
EventNexa allows users to post and view events, giving the opportunity for individuals to see what's going on in their respective locations.
By providing a platform for users to see what events are going on where we hope that this will promote individuals to attend such events.
In it's current stage the website is not secure and as such we reccomend not entering any sensitive information.
We hope you enjoy the website and utilize it to seek out like minded people with similiar interests to yourself.
Best wishes - The EventNexa team
