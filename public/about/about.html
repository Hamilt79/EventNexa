About


EventNexa is a website crafted from three students at Miami University.
The goal of EventNexa is to provide a platform that promotes human interaction.
EventNexa allows users to post events they are hosting as well as to join events other users have posted.
With each event a location is added as well as a time allowing all those interested to see when and what event is taking
place in various locations around the world.
By having this website readily avalaible we hope users will utilize it in order to meet new people whom have similiar
interests, promoting human connection and to bridge the gap between the virtual and physical world.
It is important to note that in it's current development stage that this website is currently not secure.
As such we advise all users to not enter secure information but rather to craft unique login details to protect themselves.
